PolkaProof

Trust Without Identity.
PolkaProof is an autonomous on-chain protocol that enables verifiable trust without requiring identity, intermediaries, or administrative control.

Trust by Code

Guarantees are enforced by immutable smart contracts. Everything can be verified publicly on-chain.

Without Identity

No KYC. No personal data. No accounts. Trust emerges from proof, not from who you are.

Autonomous

No admin keys. No upgrades. No hidden control. The protocol works independently of any individual or organization.

Why PolkaProof exists

Crypto still relies heavily on social trust: founders, teams, and reputation. PolkaProof replaces social trust with verifiable constraints enforced by code.

PolkaProof is open-source, neutral infrastructure.
It provides no financial services, identity verification, or compliance guarantees.
