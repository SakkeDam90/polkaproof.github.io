Our Philosophy

PolkaProof is not an application.
It is neutral, permissionless infrastructure for verifiable trust.

Built to Be Built Upon

PolkaProof is designed as a foundational protocol. Developers can freely integrate it to add trust, reputation, and coordination to blockchain systems — without identity, accounts, or intermediaries.

Why This Is Exceptional

Blockchains provide transparency, but no native trust. Web2 provides trust, but only through identity and control. PolkaProof enables trust without identity — enforced by code, not by authority.

Neutral by Design

No admin keys. No discretionary control. Governance exists only to coordinate, never to dominate. This neutrality is what allows other protocols to safely build on top of PolkaProof.

Infrastructure, not authority.
